About Skillet Chicken Bulgogi

Skillet Chicken Bulgogi is a flavorful, quick-to-make dish that brings the bold, savory taste of Korean BBQ to your kitchen. Tender chicken is marinated in a sweet and spicy sauce made with soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and a touch of sesame oil, then seared in a hot skillet for a crispy finish. Perfect for busy weeknights, this dish pairs well with rice and vegetables for a satisfying meal that's both delicious and easy to prepare.

Your Ingredients

  • One fourth cup chopped onion
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 and a half tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • One half teaspoon cayenne
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into thin strips

Cooking Directions

  1. Whisk onion, soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, sesame oil, sesame seeds, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl until marinade is smooth.
  2. Cook and stir chicken and marinade together in a large skillet over medium-high heat until chicken is cooked through, about 15 minutes.
